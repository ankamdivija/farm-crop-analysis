Farming Insights

Chemical ranges for crop yield

Most crops yield optimally with a chemical application range between 0 and 75.

Optimal pH Levels

The majority of crops thrive best in soils with a pH level between 6 and 7.

Temperature and Crop Growth

  • Most crops, except for a few, achieve higher yields at temperatures ranging from 20°C to 30°C.
  • Mangoes require temperatures above 30°C, suitable for summer cultivation.

Humidity's Impact on Crop Yield

A direct correlation exists where an increase in humidity up to 100 enhances crop yield.

Specific Crop Requirements

Rice necessitates heavy rainfall and high humidity. Coconut requires high humidity levels to thrive.

Correlation Between Soil Nutrients

Phosphorous and Potassium content in the soil are highly correlated.

Nutrient Requirements

The nitrogen content needed for most crops varies from 0 to 40.
